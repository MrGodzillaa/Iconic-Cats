1. Introduction

Welcome to Iconic Cats

What is Iconic Cats?

Iconic Cats is a collection of 1000 unique, algorithmically generated cat NFTs living on the Ethereum blockchain. Each cat NFT is a unique digital collectible with various traits and rarity levels.

Project Vision

Our goal is to create a fun, engaging NFT collection that brings together cat lovers and NFT enthusiasts. We aim to build a strong community around our collection and provide ongoing value to our holders.

Key Features

Unique Collection

1000 completely unique cat NFTs with no duplicates

Community Focused

Active community with regular events and rewards

Future Utility

Planned features including New Collections and CEX Listings
